## Hard boundaries

No biometric identification, no face recognition, no individual tracking, no
targeting, no weaponization, no personal identity inference. Human review is
required before any AI output is used operationally. Full list:
[buraq-ai-integration.md §5](../buraq-ai-integration.md#5-security-boundaries).
